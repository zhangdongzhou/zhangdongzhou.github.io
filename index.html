I am a mineral physicist working at the Partnership for eXtreme Xtallography (PX^2) program, which is located at the GeoSoilEnviroCARS beamline 13-BM-C, Advanced Photon Source, Argonne National Laboratory. I am employed by the Hawai’i Institute of Geophysics and Planetology, University of Hawai’i at Manoa, and financially supported by Consortium for Materials Properties Research in Earth Sciences. I am interested in optical spectroscopy, condensed matter physics, high pressure experiment, and planetary interiors.
